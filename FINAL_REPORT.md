# Phishing Email Detection: Comprehensive Analysis
## Traditional ML vs LLM-Based Approaches

**Project Duration**: Phases 1-7
**Datasets**: Enron (3k emails), Combined (2k emails)
**Date**: February 2026

---

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive study evaluated multiple approaches for phishing email detection, from traditional machine learning to advanced multi-agent LLM systems. **Key finding: Traditional ML (Logistic Regression, Naive Bayes) significantly outperforms all LLM-based approaches**, achieving 98-99% accuracy compared to 55-97% for LLM methods.
